## AI Infrastructure

- **NVIDIA H200 production ramp accelerates.** TSMC's 3nm yield improvements unlock full capacity on latest Hopper variants; enterprise GPU backlogs begin clearing, pressuring near-term ASPs but validating long-term capacity thesis for data center expansion through 2027.

- **Figure AI closes funding at $2.6B+ valuation post-demo.** Humanoid dexterity improvements (Figure-02) generate investor interest; commercial timelines for logistics/warehousing still 2-3 years out but acceleration in vision-based control is material.

## Energy

## Crypto

- **Ginkgo Bioworks scales precision fermentation for industrial enzymes at $50B+ run-rate equivalent margin.** Synthetic biology platform model matures; SaaS-like SG&A leverage becomes visible, shifting narrative from R&D-dependent biotech to recurring revenue model.
