# Investment Brief — 2026-04-16

## AI Infrastructure
- **Memory Bottleneck Emerges as AI Scaling Accelerates**: Inference and agentic AI initiatives are creating constraints in AI semiconductor memory availability. This shift signals a transition from compute-limited to memory-limited scaling—opening opportunities in memory manufacturers and chipmakers optimizing for large-model inference architectures.

- **Semiconductor Capital Intensity Sustains**: Hyperscaler capital expenditure continues fueling record semiconductor investment, with estimates of $650B+ in combined AI infrastructure spending. The sustained cycle favors established players with manufacturing capacity (TSMC) and creates risks for marginal pure-play fabless designers.
